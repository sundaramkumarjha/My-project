Welcome to the Robotics Club of BCE Bakhtiyarpur, where innovation meets engineering excellence! As a dynamic hub for aspiring technologists and robotics enthusiasts, we're dedicated to fostering creativity, collaboration, and hands-on learning in the exciting field of robotics. Whether you're a seasoned expert or just beginning your journey, our club offers a vibrant community and a wealth of opportunities to explore, experiment, and push the boundaries of technology. Join us as we embark on a journey of discovery, innovation, and robot-building adventures!
Bakhtiyarpur College of Engineering (BCE), located in Bakhtiyarpur, Bihar, is a renowned institution dedicated to providing quality education in the field of engineering and technology. Established with the vision of imparting technical knowledge and nurturing future leaders in the industry, BCE has emerged as a prominent name in the educational landscape of Bihar.

OUR TEAM :

Our team comprises dedicated individuals driven by a passion for robotics and technology.
